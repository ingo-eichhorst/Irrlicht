# Explainer Image Prompt

Compose a **16:9 explainer image (1920×1080)** using the two provided reference images.

## Reference Images

- **Image A** (foreground, fixed asset): the menu-bar dropdown screenshot. Treat it as inviolable — preserve its pixels exactly and place it as-is. Do not redraw, recolor, restyle, or reflow any element inside it. **Center** it on the canvas, both horizontally and vertically.
- **Image B** (background atmosphere): the dark-forest-path photo. Use it as the **full-bleed background** of the canvas. Extend it to fill 1920×1080 by mirroring or generating outward from the supplied frame — keep the moody framing of dark trees on the left and right edges and the misty central clearing receding to a vanishing point. Do not invent stars, lanterns, animals, paths, characters, or signage that are not already in the source.

## Background Treatment

- Darken Image B globally by **~25%** so it reads as backdrop, not subject.
- Add a soft elliptical vignette darkening the corners further so the screenshot and the callouts have **~70% local contrast**.
- Underlay the screenshot with a near-black navy wash (`#050a14` at **~85% opacity**, blurred **~80 px**, **~80 px bleed** beyond the screenshot's edge) so the menu-bar art reads cleanly against the trees.
- The areas where the callout text sits (left and right of the screenshot) get a similar but lighter wash (`#050a14` at **~55% opacity**) so body text stays legible without obliterating the forest.
- No bright overlays, no light leaks, no aurora.
- One soft circular wisp of `#8B5CF6` at **~8% opacity** may float behind the canvas, blurred **~120 px**, like distant candlelight.
- Overlay a fine film-grain noise at **~3% opacity** across the whole canvas.

Each connector ends in a **3 px filled purple dot** on the screenshot side. Lines are orthogonal (only horizontal and vertical segments, single 90° bend allowed). Connectors do not cross.

Each callout uses two rows:

## What Not To Draw

- No mockup of a laptop, monitor, or hand.
- No 3D perspective.
- No drop shadows on text.
- No glassmorphism panels around the callouts — they sit directly on the dark wash.
- No emoji except the colored dots (●) inside callouts where a state is named.
- No ASCII flowers, sparkles, or noise patterns beyond the global film grain.
- No "Get started" or "Download" CTA — this is an explainer, not a marketing CTA.
- Do not add a colored border around the screenshot — preserve it exactly as provided.
- Do not add stars, lanterns, fog volumes, fireflies, will-o'-the-wisps, or any new figures into the forest reference; extend it only by mirroring/continuing what is already there.
- DO NOT print color annotations like: (mono dim `#5a6378`). They are purely for information.

## Type Pairing Reference

- **Cormorant Garamond** (serif, weight 300) — display title
- **Outfit** (sans 300/500) — body and callout text
- **DM Mono** (300/400) — tracked uppercase eyebrows and small data labels

UI prose is lowercase. Em-dashes (—) over colons. No exclamation marks.
